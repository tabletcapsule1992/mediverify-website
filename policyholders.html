Your Guide to Hassle-Free Claims

How to File Your Claim

Step 1: Inform Us

Call our 24/7 helpline or email us immediately after hospitalization

Step 2: Verification

We verify your policy coverage and eligibility within hours

Step 3: Documentation

Submit required documents - we guide you through each requirement

Step 4: Approval

Get quick approval and start treatment without financial worry

Step 5: Treatment

Undergo treatment at our network hospital with cashless facility

Step 6: Settlement

We handle direct settlement with hospital - no hassles for you

Required Documents

  • Hospital bills & receipts
  • Discharge summary
  • Investigation reports

  • Policy document copy
