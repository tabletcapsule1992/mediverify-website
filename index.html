How Our Claims Process Works

A structured, step-by-step approach ensuring efficiency and clarity

1

Claim Intimation

Policyholder or hospital notifies us about planned or emergency hospitalization. Immediate case registration in our system.

2

Policy Verification

Rapid verification of policy status, coverage details, sum insured, and applicable co-payments or deductibles.

3

Authorization

Pre-authorization request reviewed by our medical team. Approval communicated to hospital and policyholder within TAT.

4

Treatment Coordination

Ongoing liaison with hospital for updates, additional authorization if required, and discharge planning.

5

Settlement & Closure

Final bill verification, settlement processing with hospital, and claim closure with complete documentation.
